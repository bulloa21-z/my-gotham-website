Welcome to Gotham City

Discover the villains that make Gotham thrilling and scary.
Population: Approximatley 10 million. Mayor: Hamilton Hill.
Commissioner: James Gordon. The streets of Gotham City are filled with filthy criminals who want to rise to power and rule over Gotham. But what they don't know is that there is someone watching over the streets all around the city. Even the dark abandoned alleyways. It's the World's Greatest Detective. The Batman!!! They call him the world's most unconventional hero. He is a detective, inventor, martial artist, criminologist, and the ultimate master at crime fighting. With heroes like The Batman, Gotham has hope.
